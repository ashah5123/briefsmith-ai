# Research findings

## Market summary

As a product manager, understanding your target audience is crucial for developing products that resonate with customers and drive business growth. Effective marketing strategies can strengthen loyalty, boost repeat purchases, and grow sales. Positioning your product against competitors requires a deep understanding of your brand's unique value proposition.

## Competitor notes

- Compete on quality
- Compete on price

## Positioning angles

- Focus on the benefits of My Product
- Emphasize My Product's competitive advantages

## Proof points

- (Source #1) Effective marketing can attract new customers to a small business.
- (Source #2) A proven way to market your product is by strengthening loyalty and boosting repeat purchases.

## Risks

- Misunderstanding the target audience
- Failing to differentiate My Product from competitors
