# Research findings

## Market summary

The market for product management and team leads is competitive, with various marketing strategies being used to attract new customers. According to Source #1, learning how to effectively market domestic production can lead to increased sales. Additionally, Sources #2-5 provide insights on effective ways to market products, including targeting the right audience (Source #6), using messaging to explain product value (Source #4), and strategically positioning products against competitors (Sources #11 and 12).

## Competitor notes

- Shopify's blog provides 12 proven ways to market a product (Source #2)
- Omnisend's blog highlights strategies for marketing products successfully (Source #3)

## Positioning angles

- Targeting the right audience through jobs-to-be-done framework (Source #9)
- Using competitor positioning to differentiate offerings (Sources #11 and 12)

## Proof points

- Effective product marketing involves understanding target audiences, messaging, and strategic positioning
- Product managers can use various marketing strategies to attract new customers

## Risks

- Failure to understand the target audience can lead to unsuccessful product launches (Source #10)
- Ignoring competitor positioning can result in loss of market share
